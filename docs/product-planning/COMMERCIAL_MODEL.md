Purpose: Keep Excel as the input layer and add a Power BI reporting layer for management insight.

## Monthly Support

- Light reporting support
- Managed reporting support
- App support / change requests

Support ranges are listed in `docs/ACSA_THREE_TIER_PROPOSAL.md` and should be confirmed before sending a formal quote.

## Client Responsibilities

- Provide sample workbook structures.
- Confirm reporting period rules.
- Confirm demographic field handling.
- Confirm named users and access roles.
- Review and approve outputs.
- Own final compliance decisions.

## SkillSet Responsibilities

- Configure templates, reports and app components within agreed scope.
- Document data rules and assumptions.
- Run demos, training and handover.
- Support agreed issue and change request process.

## Change Request Process

1. Log requested change.
2. Classify as defect, small change or new scope.
3. Estimate effort and impact.
4. Get approval before build.
5. Test and release.

## Assumptions

- Client data is provided in a usable workbook format.
- Client owns compliance interpretation and final approvals.
- Production Microsoft licensing is available or procured by the client.
- Pricing is confirmed before formal issue.

## Exclusions

- Official SETA submission responsibility.
- Official B-BBEE outcome determination.
- Payroll or HR system replacement.
- Legal advice.
- Client licensing costs.
