# SkillSet SDF / WSP / ATR App - Master Product Plan

## 1. Product Vision

Create a repeatable SkillSet product that helps an SDF move from workbook-based training records to trusted WSP planning, ATR achievement review, management reporting and, in later phases, a governed Microsoft Power Platform app.

The product should start small, prove the reporting logic, then grow into a production workflow once the client data model, ownership and controls are agreed.
